Pizza POS Systems for Pizza Places

by Gerard Gust

Revel Pizza POS offers a complete point of sale platform for owners and managers of pizza restaurants. Its intuitive UI makes it easy for employees to learn and navigate. For owners, Revel’s Intelligent reporting provides up-to-the-minute updates on sales — allowing for quick decision making and peace of mind. Whether your pizza business is one location, or a franchise chain, Revel Pizza POS has the flexibility to scale to all business needs.

Features

  • Delivery management: Track driver location and efficiency in real-time
  • Online ordering: Gives customers the opportunity to order ahead online
  • Franchise management: Easily view & manage reports from multiple establishments
  • Inventory management: Keep track of your inventory & avoid waste

Keeping customers happy is a sure-fire way to boost profits and create brand ambassadors. Every Revel POS system comes with a complete CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system for keeping track of customers and their orders. Find out which customers are returning frequently and offer them custom discount and coupons. With Revel’s loyalty program integration, customers can use their mobile phones to engage with the POS system and redeem rewards. From gift cards and rewards cards to mobile apps and payment system, Revel Pizza POS come with all the ingredients you need for the perfect point of sale recipe.
